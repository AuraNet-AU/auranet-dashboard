# AuraNet Market Positioning - VPN Blocking & Social Media Ban Enforcement

## Core Positioning Statement
**"AuraNet: The Only Way to Actually Enforce Australia's Social Media Ban"**

AuraNet is positioned as the essential enforcement tool for Australian parents dealing with the government's under-16 social media ban. While the ban removed 5 million teen accounts, VPNs allow easy circumvention - and AuraNet is the only consumer product that blocks VPNs at the network level.

## The Market Opportunity

### The Social Media Ban (December 2025)
On December 10, 2025, Australia became the first country in the world to ban social media for children under 16. The ban covers:
- TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Snapchat, YouTube, X (Twitter), Reddit, Twitch, Kick, and Threads
- Platforms face fines up to AUD $30 million for non-compliance
- No parental consent exceptions allowed

**Impact:**
- 4.7 million accounts removed in the first month (Source: Prime Minister Anthony Albanese, January 2026)
- TikTok: 1.5 million accounts removed
- Meta platforms (Instagram/Facebook): 2 million accounts removed
- 25% reduction in cyberbullying reports in first month

### Parent Support & Concern

**Support for the ban:**
- **77% of Australians support the age limit** (YouGov poll, November 2024)
- Parents groups called it a "victory for common sense"
- 70% approval among families surveyed (Australian Parents Council)

**The VPN bypass problem:**
- **86% of parents say kids will get around the restrictions** (University of Sydney, Emerging Online Safety Issues Report, 2025)
- **86% of young people say they know how to bypass** (same source)
- VPN downloads surged before the ban
- Platforms are required to detect VPNs but haven't solved it yet

**Sources:**
1. YouGov opinion poll, November 2024 (referenced in Wikipedia: Online Safety Amendment Act)
2. Dr. Justine Humphry, University of Sydney Emerging Online Safety Issues Report (quoted in TechRadar, December 2025)
3. eSafety Commissioner data (January 2026)

## The Gap AuraNet Fills

### Current Solutions DON'T Work

**Social media platforms (Instagram, TikTok, etc.):**
- Removed 5 million accounts but can't detect VPNs effectively
- Cross-reference IP with GPS, but kids use fake locations
- Behavioral analysis easily fooled

**Router parental controls (TP-Link, NetGear, ASUS):**
- Can block social media domains
- CANNOT block VPN apps effectively
- Kids download VPN, bypass everything
- No network-level DNS blocking

**Manual monitoring:**
- Parents can't watch 24/7
- Kids are more tech-savvy
- Destroys trust between parent and child

### Why AuraNet Is The Only Real Solution

**Network-level VPN blocking:**
1. Blocks VPN app downloads at DNS level (app.nordvpn.com, expressvpn.com, etc.)
2. Blocks known VPN server IP ranges
3. Blocks social media even if VPN somehow works
4. No way to bypass without the WiFi password

**Enforcement without conflict:**
- "It's not me, it's the government - I'm just following the law"
- AuraNet is the technical enforcement mechanism
- Parents stay the good guy

**Visibility & peace of mind:**
- Weekly email: "This week AuraNet blocked 47 VPN download attempts"
- Real-time alerts: "Emma's phone just tried to download NordVPN"
- Historical logs showing compliance
