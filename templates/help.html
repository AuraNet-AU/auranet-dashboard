Getting Started

Choose a Protection Mode
On the Home page, select Kids Mode for maximum protection, Family Mode for balanced protection, or Standard Mode for basic ad-blocking and security.
Assign Devices to Groups (Optional)
Go to the Devices page to give different protection levels to different devices. For example, put your children's tablets in "Kids Devices" and your laptop in "Adult Devices".
You're Protected!
That's it! Every device connected to your home WiFi is now protected. The protection works automatically - you don't need to install anything on your phones, tablets, or computers.

Common Questions

A website I need is being blocked. What do I do?
Go to the Home page, scroll to "Allow or Block a Website", enter the website address (like "example.com"), and click Allow. The site will be unblocked within a few minutes.
I allowed/blocked a site but nothing changed?
Your browser and devices remember website addresses for a short time. Try:
  • Wait 5 minutes and try again
  • Close and reopen your browser completely
  • Restart the device (phone, tablet, or computer)
Why would I pause protection?
Sometimes a website you trust might be incorrectly blocked. Instead of pausing (which removes ALL protection), we recommend using the "Allow" feature to unblock just that specific site. Only pause protection as a last resort.
Does AuraNet slow down my internet?
No! In fact, it often makes your internet feel faster because it blocks ads and trackers before they even load. You'll notice web pages load quicker and use less data.
Do I need to install anything on my devices?
No! AuraNet protects your entire network automatically. Any device that connects to your home WiFi is protected - phones, tablets, computers, smart TVs, game consoles, everything.
What's the difference between protection modes?
  • Kids Mode: Blocks adult content, social media, gambling, gaming, dangerous sites, and ads
  • Family Mode: Blocks adult content, gambling, dangerous sites, and ads
  • Standard Mode: Blocks dangerous sites and ads only
Can my kids bypass AuraNet?
As long as they're connected to your home WiFi, they're protected. However, if they use mobile data (4G/5G) instead of WiFi, they won't be protected. For complete protection, you can turn off mobile data on their devices.

Troubleshooting

The internet isn't working at all
  1. Check if your main router has internet (try connecting directly to it)
  2. Unplug your AuraNet device, wait 30 seconds, plug it back in
  3. Wait 2-3 minutes for it to fully start up
  4. If still not working, check the System Health page
The status shows "Unprotected"
This means protection is paused or disabled. Go to the Home page and click Enable Protection to turn it back on.
Ads are still showing up
Some ads (especially on YouTube and Facebook) are difficult to block because they come from the same servers as the actual content. AuraNet blocks most ads, but not 100% of them. For better ad blocking on specific apps, consider using their premium ad-free versions.
